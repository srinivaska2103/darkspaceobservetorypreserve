Our Legacy

How It All Began

1998

The first telescope was planted in this soil.

A Pure Love for Astronomy

It started with a single observer and a dream to share the quiet majesty of the universe with others. We believe everyone deserves to see a galaxy with their own eyes.

The Fight Against Light Pollution

As cities grew, the stars faded. We realized that natural darkness is a vanishing resource that must be actively protected through strict conservation and policy.

An Immersive Vision

Today, Stellaris is more than an observatory; it's an educational sanctuary designed to immerse you in the science and spirit of the cosmos.

Core Philosophy

Our Mission

Moving beyond observation into the realm of active preservation and cosmic literacy.

Preserve Natural Dark Skies

Protecting the integrity of the night by minimizing artificial light and maintaining pristine ecological conditions.

Promote Astronomy Education

Igniting curiosity through workshops, research programs, and accessible scientific dialogue for all ages.

Provide Unforgettable Experiences

Crafting bespoke stargazing moments that connect the human spirit to the vastness of the cosmos.

Raise Awareness on Light Pollution

Educating communities on the health, environmental, and cultural impacts of excessive artificial lighting.

The Stellaris Distinction

What Makes Us Special

We combine research-grade technology with a deep commitment to environmental stewardship.

Certified Dark Sky Location

Recognized by international bodies for our commitment to zero light pollution and pristine viewing conditions.

Observatory Telescopes

Access research-grade tracking telescopes that reveal deep-sky nebulae and distant galaxies in high definition.

Expert Astronomers

Every session is led by professional guides who turn complex astrophysics into an engaging, human story.

Eco-friendly Lighting

Our facility uses motion-sensor, red-light technology to protect nocturnal wildlife and visitor night vision.

Ideal Remote Location

Positioned 2,500 feet above sea level and miles away from city lights, we offer the clearest atmospheric transparency in the region.
